This blueprint outlines a realistic, scalable, and highly automated ecommerce ecosystem designed to:

- Generate products
- Market products

### Multi-agent structure

1. **Research Agent** — Finds trends, keywords, products, and viral content
2. **Content Agent** — Creates posts, blogs, ads, scripts, captions
3. **Design Agent** — Creates thumbnails, mockups, product covers
4. **Publishing Agent** — Publishes to TikTok, YouTube, Pinterest, Instagram, Shopify
5. **Sales Agent** — Optimizes funnels, emails, landing pages
6. **Analytics Agent** — Tracks ROI, conversion, and viral metrics
